# **1. Introduction**

## Organizations have unstructured knowledge, fragmented documents, or slow expert workflows. Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) shines wherever people repeatedly ask questions whose answers already exist somewhere in your internal documents — but are hard to find.

## Here are some highest‑ROI use cases for RAG:
### 1. IT Support & Troubleshooting
 - RAG can unify Confluence pages, Jira tickets, Runbooks, SOPs, Knowledge bases, Slack threads, and give instant, accurate answers.

### 2. Legal & Compliance Knowledge Retrieval
 - RAG can surface contract clauses, regulatory requirements, policy interpretations, precedent summaries, risk assessments, and pair with chunk‑level citations.

### 3. Engineering Knowledge & DevOps Runbooks
 - RAG becomes a “unified engineering brain” that answers questions for architecture diagrams, API docs, deployment steps, incident postmortems, internal libraries, configuration guides.

### 4. Finance & Operations SOPs
 - RAG can turn procedural knowledge, such as procurement rules, expense policies, vendor onboarding, audit documentation, forecasting models, into instant answers.

### 5. Research & Competitive Intelligence
 - RAG helps summarize, compare, and extract insights from market research, analyst reports, scientific papers, competitor filings, product specs, etc.

### 6. Customer Support & Help Centers
 - RAG can unify product manuals, troubleshooting guides, FAQs, Zendesk tickets, training docs.

### 7. Product Management & Requirements Intelligence
 - RAG can search across PRDs, design docs, user research, roadmap notes, Slack discussions, etc.

### 8. Healthcare & Life Sciences Knowledge Retrieval
 - RAG helps clinicians and researchers find relevant information fast in clinical guidelines, trial protocols, medical literature, drug interaction databases.

### 9. Manufacturing & Field Service Manuals
 - RAG can answer questions regarding equipment manuals, maintenance logs, repair procedures, safety protocols.

### 10. HR Policy Q&A & Employee internal Training & Onboarding
 - RAG can unify employee handbook, benefits PDFs, policy documents, intranet pages, becomes a personalized onboarding and learning assistant, and give instant, accurate answers with citations.
